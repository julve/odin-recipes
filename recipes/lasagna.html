Easy Lasagna

Description

This recipe covers how to prepare lasagna at home and in a simple way without overlooking the taste. Ingredients are easy to get and you don't need to be a chef to cook it.

The advantage of this recipe is that most ingredients can be bought on a superstore instead of preparing them by yourself, saving you some time.

Ingredients

  • Beef
  • Spaghetti sauce
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Dried parsley
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Water

Steps

The step-by-step recipe is below, however the summarized steps are the following:

  1. Cook and drain the ground beef, then stir in the spaghetti sauce and simmer.
  2. Combine the cottage cheese, 2 cups of mozzarella, eggs, half of the Parmesan, and seasonings.
  3. Assemble the lasagna according to the detailed recipe
  4. Bake, covered, for 45 minutes.
  5. Uncover and continue baking for 10 minutes.
